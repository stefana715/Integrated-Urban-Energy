# Integrated Urban Retrofit + Solar Deployment under Climate Change

**Paper 3** — City-scale assessment of integrated building retrofit and rooftop solar deployment potential under climate change: Changsha, China

## Overview

This project integrates outputs from two completed studies into a city-scale urban energy analysis:

- **Paper 1** (Solar Screening): OSM-based solar potential screening of 18,855 buildings in Changsha's urban core, identifying 6,411 high-potential buildings and 146 priority 500 m grid cells.
- **Paper 2** (Retrofit Strategies): EnergyPlus archetype simulations for three building eras (Era 1/2/3) with five retrofit packages (R1–R5) under current and future climate scenarios (CMIP6 morphing, SSP245/SSP585, 2050/2080).
